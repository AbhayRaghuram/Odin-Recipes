Bulletproof Coffee


Bulletproof Coffee is a rich, creamy coffee drink filled with healthy fats. It’s made with coffee, grass-fed butter, and MCT oil. Coffee with butter has many added health benefits that you don’t get from regular black coffee. It’s popular in the keto and paleo communities, and is said to help with weight loss. Learn how to make bulletproof coffee and make this your new morning fuel.

Recipe Ingredients:

  • 12 Ounces Bulletproof brand Brewed Coffee Hot
  • 2 Tablespoons Ghee
  • 1 Tablespoon MCT oil

    • Optional Additions:

  • Pinch Pink Himalayan Salt
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Ground Cinnamon
  • Stevia
  • Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
  • Protein Powders
  • Sugar Free Syrups
  • Collagen Peptides
  • Heavy Cream

Preparation Instructions:

Prep Time: 10 Mins, Cook Time: 10 Minutes, Serves: 1

  1. Place All Ingredients in a Blender and Blend Until Fully Combined and Smooth.
  2. Serve Immediately

    3. Enjoy!

Nutrition Information:

Calories: 268kcal (13%) Fat: 29g (45%) Saturated Fat: 18g (113%) Cholesterol: 76mg (25%) Sodium: 1170mg (51%) Potassium: 166mg (5%)
